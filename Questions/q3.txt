Question: What will be the output of the following code snippet?
Options: 
1. al
2. ale
3. cal
4. caler

Answer: 1 (al)
Explaination: The substring function will count from index 2 to 3 (4th index will be excluded). The character present at 2 and 3 are "a" and "l" respectively. So the answer is "al".
