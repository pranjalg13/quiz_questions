Question: 
What is the use of the <noscript> tag in Javascript?

Options: 
1. The contents are displayed by non-JS-based browsers.
2. Clears all the cookies and cache.
3. Both A and B
4. None

Answer: 1 (The contents...)
Explaination: The <noscript> tag defines an alternate content to be displayed to users that have disabled scripts in their browser
